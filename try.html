Souffle Pancake

Soufflé pancakes are thick, fluffy, and airy pancakes.
They have a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture and are often topped with powdered sugar, fruits, whipped cream, or syrup.

German Pancake

A German pancake, also known as a Dutch baby, is a large, fluffy, oven-baked pancake that puffs up dramatically while cooking.
It has a crispy, golden-brown edge and a soft, custard-like center.

Crepes are thin, delicate pancakes with a soft, flexible texture.
